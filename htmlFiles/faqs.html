Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What is the SLIET Mentorship Platform?

The SLIET Mentorship Platform connects students with faculty mentors to provide academic and career guidance.

2. How can I book a slot with a mentor?

You can book a slot by navigating to the "Home" page, selecting a mentor, and clicking the "Book Slot" button.

3. Can I book multiple slots with the same mentor?

No, you can only book one slot per mentor to ensure equal access for all students.

4. What if I need to cancel a booking?

You can cancel your booking by going to the "Booked Slots" page and clicking the "Cancel" button next to your booking.
